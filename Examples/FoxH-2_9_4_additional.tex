~\cite{kilbas.saigo:04:h-transforms}:

  \FoxH
    {1,0}
    {0,1}
    {z}
    {}
    {\left(b, \beta\right)}
    = \frac{1}{\beta} z^{b/\beta} \exp\left(-z^{1 /\beta}\right).
\end{align*}
